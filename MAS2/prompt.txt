nverts is txt that on each line has number of simplices in clique. Times has year per row first row in nvert corresponds to first row in times.

For example nverts looks like this:
2
3

and simplices look like this
1
2
3
8
9

and times look like this:
2018
2019

In total we will have two cliques, first one will have two members with ID-> 1,2 it will be for year 2018 and second clique will have ids with -> 3,8,9 it will be for year 2019, each year has its own network
